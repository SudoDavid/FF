Our Mission

At Florida Futurists, our vision is to be a driving force in shaping a dynamic and prosperous future for the Sunshine State. We envision a Florida that thrives on innovation, embraces sustainable practices, and leverages cutting-edge technologies to propel economic growth, social progress, and environmental stewardship.

Leadership and Beoming An Regional or City Leader

There are a total of 9 main areas in Florida and we believe that we should have a
representative from each area, so those regions are intended to house an area leader
who is elected each year to participate in meetings each year, along with our Galactic members
and employees. Together we will shape the direction of Florida's Future and ensure that any
means necessary will be used to safegaurd a brighter tomorrow.
